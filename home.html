Features

What we offer

Team of Experts

Our skilled and experienced pilots are dedicated to capturing the best possible shots of your porperty. All fliers are FAA-certified and have extensive training in drone flight, navigation, and photography.

High-Quality Drones

We use only the latest and most advanced equipment to capture high quality videos and images. The drones are fitted with high-resolution cameras, stabilizers, and obstacle avoidance to ensure safe flights that produce stunning footage.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We're confident in our ability to provide the best possible services. But if for any reason you are not satisfied with the content provided, you won't be charged for our services.

How it works

Start flying in 3 simple steps

01

Tell us what you're looking for

To get started, simply fill out our contact form and tell us exactly what you're looking for. Aside from what's included in the standard package, which can be found below, feel free to let us know if there are any specific angles or shots you'd like us to capture.

02

We'll plan the shoot

After receiving your request, we'll create a plan specifically tailored to your property that is sure to attract potential buyers. You'll then be provided with a detailed outline of our services and pricing.

03

Get your footage

It will usually take our pilots 45 minutes to capture all of the pictures and videos for a shoot. After it's complete, we'll edit and enhance all of the media to provide you with stunning, high-quality content that showcases your property in the best light.

Whats included

What you'll get from a shoot

Photographs

At least 16 pictures in 4k resolution that will capture every angle of your property in addition to the surrounding areas.

Videos

At least 12 cinematic videos shot in 4k resolution at 60 frames per second (or 30 frames per second with HDR).

Extras

If there are any other custom shots that you'd like to add (or any standard ones you'd like to remove), let us know and we'll make it happen.

Editing

We'll fully edit all of the pictures and videos to give them a professional look before sending them off to you and your clients.
